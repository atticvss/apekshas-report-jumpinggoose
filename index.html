01 / THE STUDIO

JG

A place where ideas find their visual voice.

JUMPINGGOOSE is a Bangalore-based strategic design and branding agency. Its work spans trend forecasting, brand identity, and retail and apparel design—turning considered ideas into visual worlds.

During this internship, I followed that process from research and early concept thinking through moodboards, Illustrator exploration, and apparel tech-pack development.
